## What You'll Build

An AI assistant that negotiates car purchases (or any big-ticket item) over email on your behalf. It researches fair market value, crafts persuasive counteroffers, and handles the tedious back-and-forth until you get the price you want.

## Why This Works

Car dealerships count on you being emotional, impatient, or uninformed. An AI is none of those things. It will:

- Research the exact market value using KBB, Edmunds, and recent sales
- Never get tired of going back and forth
- Stay calm, professional, and firm
- Walk away without flinching (the most powerful negotiation move)

## Prerequisites

- OpenClaw running on your machine
- Email access configured (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
- A car you want to buy (or any vendor negotiation)

## Step 1: Give Your AI the Context

Tell your AI what you're buying and what you know. Be specific:

> "I'm buying a 2024 Toyota RAV4 XLE AWD. The dealer quoted $38,500. KBB fair purchase price is $34,800. I'd like to get it for $34,000-35,000. The dealer's name is Mike at Springfield Toyota. His email is mike@springfieldtoyota.com."

## Step 2: Let It Research

Your AI will pull current market data, check inventory levels in your area, and find any manufacturer incentives or rebates you might not know about. This becomes its ammunition.

## Step 3: Draft the Opening Email

The AI crafts a professional but firm opening offer, referencing specific data points. No bluffing, just facts:

- Fair market value from multiple sources
- Current inventory levels (high inventory = more leverage)
- Competing offers from other dealers
- Any applicable incentives

## Step 4: Handle the Back-and-Forth

This is where the AI shines. Dealers will counter, deflect, add fees, or try urgency tactics. Your AI:

- Responds within minutes (or on whatever schedule you set)
- Never takes things personally
- References data in every response
- Knows when to hold firm vs. when to concede small points
- Can contact multiple dealers simultaneously to create real competition

## Step 5: Close the Deal

Once you hit your target range, the AI flags it for your approval before confirming. You stay in control of the final yes/no.

## The Results

Duncan Rogoff saved **$4,200** on his car purchase using this exact approach. The AI handled dozens of emails across multiple dealerships over several days, something most people don't have the patience for.

## Tips

- **Works for anything**: vendor contracts, SaaS renewals, freelancer rates, home repairs
- **Multi-dealer strategy**: Have your AI email 5-10 dealers simultaneously. Real competition beats fake leverage every time
- **Set boundaries**: Tell your AI your walk-away price upfront
- **Review before sending**: Start with approval mode where you see every email before it goes out. Once you trust the tone, let it fly

## Beyond Cars

Once you see this work, you'll want it everywhere. Annual SaaS renewals? Insurance quotes? Contractor bids? Any negotiation where the other side expects you to give up is a negotiation your AI will win.
